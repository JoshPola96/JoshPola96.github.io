Deep Learning Model

This site is intended to the front-end for a trained ML model. It utilizes datasets from Waseem & Hovy's GitHub and Kaggle, which are processed, cleaned, and divided into numerical and textual datasets. Textual data goes through a BERT encoder, serving a CNN model and bi-LSTM classifier. The numerical dataset trains a neural network, and both models are combined at a final classification layer.
